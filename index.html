Maha Shivaratri

May Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati bless our journey together
with eternal love, divine grace, and infinite happiness

Just as Shiva and Parvati are inseparable,
you are the divine blessing in my life.
On this sacred night, I thank the universe
for bringing you into my world.
